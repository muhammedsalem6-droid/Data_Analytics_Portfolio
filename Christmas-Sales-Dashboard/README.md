# Christmas Sales Performance Dashboard

## Business Objective
Analyze Christmas season sales performance to understand revenue drivers,
profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX

## Key Insights
- 1.16M total revenue with 31% profit margin
- Food & Sweets, Lights, Decorations are top-performing categories
- Central and East regions lead seasonal revenue
- High-value customers generate strong revenue with fewer transactions

## Recommendations
- Increase inventory and staffing during peak holiday days
- Focus promotions on top-performing categories and regions
- Introduce loyalty offers for repeat seasonal customers
